  • Travel tips
  1. Have a travel plan.
  2. Use a small backpack/suitcase.
  3. Only bring what you need.
  4. Only wear your most comfortable shoes.
  5. Photocopy your driver’s license, passport, visa, vaccination status (if required), and other important documents.
  6. Buy all your adapters, SIM cards, and currency at home.
  7. Call your phone carrier to add international service to your plan if necessary.
  8. Even if you are somewhat fluent in the language spoken in the country you're visiting, bring along a phrasebook for those times when you may get stumped.
  9. Be careful and don’t take any risk.
  10. Take an extra credit card with you.
